The Chesa Forest story

A protected boundary is only a line—until evidence gives it a voice.

Chesa State Forest appears green from a distance. Look closer and a different geography emerges: cropland fragments the protected landscape, grass and shrub cover dominate, and historical tree-cover loss records where pressure has already left a mark.

1 · Detect pressureLand cover reveals agriculture, bare ground and vegetation states requiring verification.
2 · Read conditionAnnual mean NDVI and NBR describe relative vegetation vigour and moisture condition in 2025.
3 · Direct actionRestoration priorities translate evidence into protection, monitoring and field decisions.
